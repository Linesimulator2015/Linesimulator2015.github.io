Everyone loves waiting in line, that's a given, so we brought this amazing experience to the safety and comfort of your living room!

Line Simulator 2015 is a game for everyone, those who love controlled, cinematic experiences, and for those who love a completely non-linear experience with no end goal. Some lines are infinite! Others are filled with QTE's and excellent voice acting. No matter what kind of gamer you are, you will love Line Simulator 2015. I *promise you will not be let down! *promise not guaranteed
